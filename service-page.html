STORIES

The Batavia Gallery houses a variety of different exhibits. These exhibits all hold different stories, and with exhibits always changing and being refreshed to improve your experience, some of the stories may be lost. Stay up to date with new and old stories.

Behind the Scenes

See and hear first-hand how a number of objects and artefacts are treated and conserved as you are led on a private viewing through maritime archaeology and conservation work areas.

Days: Saturday and Sunday
Time: 10.30am - 11.30am
Cost: Free

The Highlights

Make the most of your visit to the WA Shipwrecks Museum with a free tour of our galleries. This tour will introduce you to some iconic objects showcased in the Museum.

Days: Every Day
Time: 10.30am & 2.30pm
Cost: Free

Object & Memories

Join us on a special tour where storytelling with objects encourages conversations and evokes memories. This is a specialised tour for people living with dementia and their carers.

Days: Everyday
Time: Call for bookings
Cost: Free

Please Touch

This tour is designed for visitors who are blind or vision impaired. Participants have the chance to touch and examine specially selected objects from the Museum's collections and displays.

Days: Everyday
Time: Call for bookings
Cost: Free
